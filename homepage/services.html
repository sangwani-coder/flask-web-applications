Kick n Fit Kids

Black Belt Academy will help your child develop essential skills they can apply to all areas of life.
You want your child to have the tools to succeed in life, but protecting them from peer pressure, bullying and predators can make any parent feel overwhelmed with anxiety and stress. The good news is that at BBA, our kids martial art program is the perfect champion to help you raise a strong, confident leader, teaching confidence, focus & self-defense skills.

Adults Martial Arts

At BBA we don't waste time with katas or forms that are interesting to watch but offer no value in a real conflict. We don't teach you how to win in the ring or make it to the Olympics. We don't practice ancient rituals. We teach you how to survive a violent encounter through practical, reality based martial arts training and self-defense classes for adults.

Defense Training

Krav maga is the world's best self-defense and fighting system dveloped for the Israel Defense Forces derived from a combination of techniques sourced from aikido, boxing, wrestling, judo, and karate. At BBA academy no experience is required for you to join in this program. At BBA we've developed this program for secuirty officers, police officers and soldiers, however, Everyone is welcome to try out this class.

Fitness Training

Blow off stress while burning off calories with this cardio kickboxing workout. Cardio kickboxing is a group fitness class that combines martial arts techniques with fast-paced cardio. This high-energy workout challenges the beginner and elite athlete alike. Build stamina, improve coordination and flexibility, and burn calories as you build lean muscle with this fun and challenging workout.
